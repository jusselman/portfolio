JOSHUA USSELMAN

Greetings, I'm Joshua Usselman, a passionate Full Stack Developer, skilled Front End Engineer, and dedicated Educator driven to craft responsive, intuitive, and visually stunning user interfaces. Hailing originally from Portland, Oregon, I have honed my expertise while working in diverse locations such as Palma de Mallorca, Guadalajara, and currently, San Francisco. My proficiency spans a wide range of technologies, including React, MongoDB, ExpressJS, NodeJS, Flutter, JavaScript, HTML5, CSS, SASS, as well as proficiency in Photoshop and Figma. With a commitment to continuous learning and a keen eye for detail, I take pride in delivering top-notch solutions that resonate with users and leave a lasting impact.
